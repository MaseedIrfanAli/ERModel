Normalization Analyzer

Select a dataset or draw entities, then click a Normal Form to check your schema and get AI feedback on how to normalize it.

1NF First Normal Form

No repeating groups, atomic values, single-valued columns only.

2NF Second Normal Form

Meets 1NF + no partial dependency (non-key attrs fully depend on the whole PK).

3NF Third Normal Form

Meets 2NF + no transitive dependency (non-key attrs depend only on PKs).

BCNF Boyce-Codd NF

Stricter 3NF — every determinant must be a candidate key.
