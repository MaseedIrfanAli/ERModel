Entities

Rectangle = strong entity. Double border = weak entity (depends on another).

Attributes

Underline = Primary Key
Purple = Foreign Key
Dashed = Multi-valued
Plain = Regular attr

Cardinality

1:1 — One to One
1:N — One to Many
M:N — Many to Many
